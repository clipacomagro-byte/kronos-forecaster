Step 1 · Input
It reads recent price history
Kronos looks at the last 300 daily candles (Open, High, Low, Close, Volume) for your chosen instrument. That's about 14 months of trading data. It doesn't read news, earnings, or fundamentals — just raw price action.
Step 2 · Tokenise
It converts prices into a language
Just like ChatGPT breaks sentences into words, Kronos breaks price bars into "tokens" — small chunks that represent patterns like "three green candles followed by a doji." This lets it treat markets like a language it can read.
Step 3 · Predict
It completes the pattern
Like autocomplete on your phone, Kronos predicts what tokens (price bars) are most likely to come next — based on every pattern it learned from millions of historical candles across crypto, stocks, and commodities.
Step 4 · Output
It shows a probability range
The gold shaded zone is NOT one prediction — it's the range across multiple runs (samples). Wide band = the model is unsure. Narrow band = higher confidence. The center line is the average expected path.
Green/Red candles — actual historical price (what really happened)
Gold line — Kronos predicted path (most likely outcome)
Gold shaded zone — uncertainty range (price could land anywhere in here)
Blue dashed line — where forecast starts (today)
⚠️ What this is NOT: Kronos does not know about upcoming earnings, Fed meetings, geopolitical events, or news. It is a pure pattern-matching model. Always combine it with the headlines and signals below — never trade on the chart alone.
